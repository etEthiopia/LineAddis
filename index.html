Who we are

Line Addis is a privately-owned international network enterprise formed by independent educational, financial and IT companies in order to provide a comprehensive educational and financial solutions for Ethiopian students. We are Based in Ethiopia, Addis Ababa, fully licensed by the Government of Ethiopia and Authorized by higher education sectors to best assist students with the quality and accessibility of education.
