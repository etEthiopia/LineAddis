Who we are

Line Addis is a private company which provide comprehensive educational solutions for Ethiopian students. We are based in Ethiopia, Addis Ababa, fully licensed by the Government of Ethiopia and authorized by the higher education sectors to best assist students in ensuring the quality and accessibility of education.
